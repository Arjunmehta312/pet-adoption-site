About Us

Pet Adoption Showcase is dedicated to connecting loving homes with animals in need. Our mission is to reduce the number of animals in shelters by facilitating adoptions and promoting responsible pet ownership.

Founded in 2024 by Arjun Mehta, Jal Bafana, Jay Desai and Jinay Jain we work closely with local shelters and rescue organizations to showcase their animals and increase adoption rates.
