Frequently Asked Questions

How do I adopt a pet?

To adopt a pet, browse our available pets, submit an application, and our team will review it. If approved, you'll meet the pet and finalize the adoption.

What are the adoption fees?

Adoption fees vary depending on the animal and its age. Fees typically cover vaccinations, spay/neuter, and initial health checks.

Can I return an adopted pet?

We have a 30-day return policy. If the adoption doesn't work out, you can return the pet, but we encourage working with our behaviorists first to address any issues.

How does pet sponsorship work?

Pet sponsorship allows you to financially support a specific animal's care without adopting. Your donation helps cover food, medical care, and other necessities.

Do you offer international adoptions?

Currently, we only facilitate adoptions within the country due to legal and logistical complexities of international pet transport.
